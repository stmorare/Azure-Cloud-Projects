
**E**

**Elasticity**: is the ability of a cloud computing system to automatically scale its resources up or down in response to real-time changes in demand.

**S**:

**Scalability**: is the ability to increase or decrease computing resources, like servers and storage, to match demand.

**W**

**Workload**: is any application, service, or computational task that runs on and consumes cloud-based resources. It can be a virtual machine, database, microservice, or a set of processes designed to perform a specific function, like data processing, hosting applications, or running complex analytics.
